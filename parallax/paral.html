Beijing

北京市

Beijing, the great capital of China. Home to to over 21.5 million inhabitants. The bustling city is filled with those who have made it and those willing to take a chance. Only the very best make it to the top. This had held true throughout its expansive history stretching over 3 millenia. The connection of the modern and past can't be seen anywhere else in the world

Shanghai

上海市

Even though Beijing might sometimes steal its thunder, Shanghai is a true monolithic giant. Shanghai is the largest city in not only China, not only Asia, but in the world standing at just under 25 million inhabitants and a gross metropolitan product of over $1.9 trillion. It has the most busy ports, airports and streets. Bustling can't even begin describe Shanghai. It is truly the show piece of China showing off everything and the best of what they have to offer.

Tianjin

天津市

While this is China's 4th most populous city you have to remember Tianjin still has a massive population of nearly 14 million inhabitants. Known as the walled city due to its origin of being built upon a walled river, the name couldn't be further from the truth. With over 500 fortune 500 companies based there it's economic power can be felt globally. Many nicely call it "China's Manhattan". Even through Earthquakes, explosions and more, Tianjin remains strong and on top.
